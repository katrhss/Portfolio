Timeline

2023

2023 is the year I started to get more deeply involved in programming. I worked with Anastasia Magopoulou on a project where we developed a mobile application for the 4th programming competition "Kozani 2030: On the road to climate neutrality". The app was developed using React-Native, which gave me not only a first look at mobile development, but also a general knowledge of React, which is the goal of my year. At the beginning of the academic year I had the honour to become the President of the IEEE Student Branch University of Western Macedonia, Kastoria. During the year, together with the other members of the Student Branch Board, we organised several workshops and seminars, including a seminar on LaTeX, which was held in cooperation with the School of Science of the University. Furthermore, I had the opportunity to participate in the 16th IEEEXtreme Programming Contest, which helped me to improve my knowledge of JavaScript, which was my preferred language. It also helped me develop better algorithmic thinking.
